framework.Attributes.Geo.bearing.calculate_bearing(pointA, pointB)[source]¶

Description: Calculates the bearing between two points. The formulae used is the following:

θ = atan2(sin(Δlong).cos(lat2),

cos(lat1).sin(lat2) − sin(lat1).cos(lat2).cos(Δlong))

  • Input:

    • pointA - latitude and longitude from initial point

    • pointB - latitude and longitude from final point

  • Outputs:

    • compass_bearing [deg]
